Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Mastoor is a private family messaging application developed by Machkit LTD. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

We built Mastoor with a privacy-first approach. Our core principle is simple: your conversations belong to you and no one else.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Username (chosen by you, no real name required)
  • Display name (chosen by you)
  • Messages and media (stored locally on your device only)

✓ We Do NOT Collect

  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Real name or identity
  • Location data
  • Contacts list
  • Browsing history
  • Biometric data

2.2 Technical Information

  • Device token for push notifications
  • Public encryption key (used for end-to-end encryption only)
  • Subscription status (active/inactive — no payment details)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Messages and Media

🔐 All messages and media are end-to-end encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption. Messages are encrypted on your device before transmission, transmitted in encrypted form only, stored locally on recipient devices, and never stored on our servers in readable form. We cannot read your messages under any circumstances.

3.2 Username and Display Name

Used solely to identify you within your family group. Your username is visible only to members of groups you join.

3.3 Push Notifications

Your device token is used solely to deliver push notifications. We do not use it for tracking or advertising.

3.4 Subscriptions

Subscription management is handled by a third-party provider. We receive only subscription status information. We do not receive or store payment information.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All messages, media, and conversation history are stored locally on your device using an encrypted database. This data never leaves your device in readable form.

4.2 Server Storage

Our servers store only:

  • Your username and display name
  • Your public encryption key
  • Group membership information
  • Encrypted message payloads (temporary relay only)

4.3 Encryption

We use industry-standard X25519 key exchange and AES-256-GCM encryption — the same standard used by governments and financial institutions worldwide. Your private encryption key is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

4.4 Security Features

  • App Lock with password protection
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)
  • Termination password to instantly wipe all local data
  • Disappearing messages with automatic deletion
  • Admin recovery code for group access recovery

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except the following service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Cloud database provider — message relay and user data storage
  • Push notification service provider — delivering notifications only
  • Subscription management provider — subscription status only
  • Apple — App Store and in-app purchase processing

5.1 Legal Requirements

⚠️ We may disclose information if required by law. However, due to our end-to-end encryption architecture, we are technically unable to provide readable message content even if legally required to do so.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Messages

Messages are stored locally on your device. You control deletion through manual deletion, disappearing messages feature, termination password, or deleting the app.

6.2 Account Data

Your username, display name, and public key remain in our database until you delete your account.

6.3 Account Deletion

To delete your account, contact us at privacy@machkit.com. We will remove your data from our servers within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Mastoor is designed for family use including children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents and guardians are responsible for supervising their children's use of the app.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@machkit.com

9. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

  • Legal basis for processing: Legitimate interest and consent
  • Data Controller: Machkit LTD
  • You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, access their personal information, and request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Mastoor after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
